In the heart of Punjab, where traditions run as deep as the five rivers, the world celebrates the beginning of #AKSHIM. A journey of a thousand lifetimes, rooted in authentic Hindu rituals and royal Punjabi vibrance, brought together by the blessings of the Marwah family.
